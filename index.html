Where's jackie at?

Hi I'm Jackie.

This is travel blog round two in which I put actual pictures that I took with a good camera that doesn't get ruined by Tumblr's compression. Okay, just kidding, my pictures aren't that great, but I wanted somewhere better to share them and to also play around with CSS, Javascript, and HTML. And thus, Where's Jackie At 2.0 was born.

Pacific Northwest Roadtrip >

In which Vivian, Jose, and I drove up the West Coast and I drove back down.

Because what better way to start the summer than a roadtrip through some of the most hipster places in the country? I had an amazing time, despite the long drives, and it was so great to experience the Pacific Northwest in the best weather for it.

East Asia Summer Trip >

In which I see Tokyo, Taipei, and Hong Kong alone and parts of Southeast China with my family.

And hopefully I don't get kidnapped while I'm at it, haha. I'm so looking forward to experiencing all the cultures and the cities by myself. Being a lone traveler is something that younger me would have never thought to do, being a female and all, but it's seriously paid off to see the world at my own pace.
